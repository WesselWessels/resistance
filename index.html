Good vs Bad



Good: 3 Bad: 2

Roles

The Oracle

A Good player who knows who the Bad players are.

Assassin

A Bad player who knows who must kill The Oracle at the end of the game to win.

Hermes

A Good player who knows who The Oracle is.

Medusa

A Bad player who appears as The Oracle to Hermes. Hermes must figure out who is the real The Oracle.

Kronos

A Bad that do not know who his fellow Bad players are, and they do not know who he is. The Oracle, however, does know that he is a Bad Player.

Hades

A Bad player that does not reveal his identity to The Oracle.
